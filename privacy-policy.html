Last Updated: February 2026 最後更新：2026年2月

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Billion Studio ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you register for an account to use our demo applications (such as Blood Pressure Tracker or Glucose Tracker), we collect:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)
  • Account creation date and last login information

1.2 Health Data (Demo Applications)

If you choose to use our health tracking applications, we may collect:

  • Blood pressure readings (systolic, diastolic, pulse)
  • Glucose levels and measurements
  • Dates and times of measurements
  • Notes or observations you add

Important: Our demo applications are for demonstration purposes only. We recommend not entering real personal health data. Any data you enter is stored securely and used solely for demonstrating application functionality.

1.3 Contact Information

When you contact us via our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Message content
  • Company name (optional)

1.4 Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics on some pages to understand how visitors interact with our website. This includes:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and screen resolution
  • Geographic location (country/city level)
  • Referral source
  • IP address (anonymized)

1.5 Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you logged into your account
  • Remember your preferences (e.g., language selection)
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Improve user experience

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and improvement
  • Hosting Providers: To store and serve our website and applications
  • Email Service Providers: To send service communications

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes or governmental requests
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Investigate potential violations of our Terms of Use
  • Protect against fraud or security threats

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure password hashing and storage
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure backup procedures

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Account data: Until you delete your account or request deletion
  • Health demo data: Until you delete it or close your account
  • Contact inquiries: For up to 2 years from last communication
  • Analytics data: Anonymized data retained indefinitely for statistical purposes

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR and Hong Kong PDPO, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain types of processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)

How to Exercise Your Rights:

Email: privacy@billionstudio.app

Response Time: We will respond to your request within 30 days

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the UK and EU
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities
  • Other legally recognized transfer mechanisms

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date

  • 電郵地址

  • 血壓讀數（收縮壓、舒張壓、脈搏）
  • 血糖水平和測量值
  • 測量的日期和時間
  • 您添加的備註或觀察

重要提示：我們的演示應用程式僅供展示用途。我們建議不要輸入真實的個人健康數據。您輸入的任何數據都會被安全儲存，僅用於展示應用程式功能。

1.3 聯絡資料

當您通過聯絡表格與我們聯繫時，我們會收集：

  • 設備類型和螢幕解析度
  • 地理位置（國家/城市級別）
  • 推薦來源

1.5 Cookies 和類似技術

我們使用 cookies 來：

  • 保持您登入帳戶

在以下情況下，我們可能會披露您的資料：

4. 數據安全

我們實施適當的技術和組織措施來保護您的個人資料，包括：

  • 傳輸中的數據加密（SSL/TLS）
  • 安全的密碼雜湊和儲存
  • 定期安全評估
  • 訪問控制和身份驗證
  • 安全備份程序

然而，沒有任何通過互聯網傳輸或電子儲存的方法是100%安全的。雖然我們努力保護您的個人資料，但我們無法保證其絕對安全。

5. 數據保留

我們保留您的個人資料，直到以下時間為止：

  • 帳戶數據：直到您刪除帳戶或要求刪除
  • 健康演示數據：直到您刪除它或關閉帳戶
